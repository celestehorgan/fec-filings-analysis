Did you know that Facebook has a PAC which donates money to various US candidates? All of its donations in the 2018 calendar year have been to Republicans, including:

  • $5000 to Devin Nunes, the chair of the House Committee on Ethics who managed to shut down their investigation into Trump's Russian dealings. Yes, that Nunes.
  • $5000 to Kevin McCarthy, who doesn't think climate change is real, and managed to get a 0% rating from Planned Parenthood and 100% from the NRA.
  • $2500 to Bob Corker, who said that he would support repealing the ACA even if it didn't come with a replacement.
